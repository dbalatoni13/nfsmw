=== GLOBAL ADDRESS HOISTING (-fforce-addr difference) ===

Our compiler hoists global variable addresses into callee-saved registers
before function calls, while the original loads them after. This creates
unnecessary register spills and larger stack frames.

  SmokeableSpawner::Init                            70.8%    56B   (TheSmackableClass addr hoisted)
  PhysicsObject::Behaviors::Add                     86.1%   272B   (gFastMem addr hoisted + code dup)
